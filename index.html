How It Works?

Maximise the investment potential of your property.

Step 1: List Your Property

Our experts give you a starting valuation. We help you take photos, do measurements and create a floorplan for your advert. We advertise your property on Rightmove, Zoopla and PrimeLocations.

Step 2: Viewings

We check for your availability and arrange viewings as an Open Day. Tenants will be able to view the appointment diary and book online slots. No call or email agents required.

Step 3: Online Auction

We invite tenants for an online auction starting at a base rent. We do reference checks on tenants before you choose a tenant from the top bidders. We also help you complete the tenancy contract and collect the deposit and first month's rent.
